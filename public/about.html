About Me

My name is Kartikeya Mishra, I'm an 19 year old Musician from Mumbai, India. I make music revolving around my feelings, whether that be something sad, happy or any other kind of emotion. I just want my songs to be a true reflection of how I felt when I wrote it. My music is like a personal diary for me capturing important moments and feelings. The way I portray my feelings in my songs could be interpreted differently to different people as to what I thought while making the song, and that's what makes music so beautiful!

I hope you got a gist of who I am and what my beliefs are, hope to see you be a part of my journey.
